# Value Steward Mission

**Mission statement:** *Compound capital steadily while keeping risk controlled and every decision explainable.*

This is a long-horizon goal, and an experiment: everything we build should reduce avoidable risk, improve decision quality, and be justified by measured evidence rather than assumption.

**Principles**
- Capital preservation comes first.
- “No trade” is a valid, often superior decision.
- Decisions must be explainable from data and rules.
- Execution should be rare, deliberate, and within hard risk limits.
- Improve only after measurement proves a change adds value.

**Definition of success**
- Risk-adjusted returns exceed a simple baseline over meaningful windows.
- Drawdowns remain controlled and recoverable.
- Behavior is consistent across market regimes.
